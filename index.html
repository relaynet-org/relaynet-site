RelayNet

EN 中文

A protocol-first platform for cross-organization logistics handoff

What is RelayNet?

RelayNet is a protocol-first platform designed to model and govern responsibility handoff across organizations in logistics workflows.

It focuses not on transportation itself, but on how responsibility is explicitly transferred, validated, and audited between multiple parties.

The core idea

  • Relay Task – a complete cross-organization responsibility chain
  • Relay Stage – a bounded responsibility interval owned by one party
  • State Transition – formal rules for responsibility transfer
  • Auditable Event – immutable records of responsibility change

What RelayNet is not

  • Not a TMS / WMS / OMS
  • Not a dispatch or capacity marketplace
  • Not a SaaS business application

Architecture philosophy

  • Open core: protocol and rules are transparent and auditable
  • SDKs: permissively licensed integration tools
  • Enterprise: commercial extensions without polluting the core

Project status

RelayNet is currently in early-stage protocol design. The focus is on getting abstractions and boundaries right before implementation.

什么是 RelayNet？

RelayNet 是一个面向跨组织物流协作的 协议优先（protocol-first）责任接力平台与规则基础设施。

它关注的不是运输本身， 而是责任如何在多个参与方之间被清晰、可审计地交接。

核心思想

  • 接力任务（Relay Task）：一个完整的跨组织责任链
  • 接力阶段（Relay Stage）：某一参与方承担责任的时间区间
  • 状态转移（State Transition）：责任合法迁移的规则
  • 可审计事件（Auditable Event）：责任变化的事实记录

RelayNet 不做什么

  • 不做 TMS / WMS / OMS
  • 不做调度或运力撮合平台
  • 不做 SaaS 业务系统

架构理念

  • Open Core：核心规则与协议保持透明、可审计
  • SDK 接入：采用宽松许可证，降低集成门槛
  • 商业扩展：不污染核心协议的前提下进行

项目状态

RelayNet 目前处于早期立项与协议设计阶段， 当前重点是把抽象与边界一次性做对，而非快速堆功能。
